上結論

Length of Input (N) Worst Accepted Algorithm
≤[10..11] O(N!),O(N6)
≤[15..18] O(2N∗N2)
≤[18..22] O(2N∗N)
≤100 O(N4)
≤400 O(N3)
≤2K O(N2∗logN)
≤10K O(N2)
≤1M O(N∗logN)
≤100M O(N),O(logN),O(1)
