ch1

Judgment & Type

A judgment is an attitude that a person takes towards expressions.
When we come to know something, we are making a judgment.
Judgments are acts of knowing, and believing is part of knowing.

有四種形式

  1. {expression} is a {type}.
  2. {expression} is the same {type} as {expression}.
  3. {type} is a type.
  4. {type} and {type} are the same type.

例子:

0 is the same Nat as 0
如果 n is the same Nat as k,
那麼 (add1 n) is the same Nat as (add1 k).

有些judgment需要其他judgment先寫好不然會看起來很尷尬

像
'pêche the same 'fruit as 'pêche
但是
'fruit is a type 很明顯不是type，他是某個Atom

Expressions that describe other expressions, such as Atom, are called types.

下面這段不是定義某個名詞，但感覺很重要所以也記在這裡

Sentences get their meaning from those who understand them.
The sentences capture thoughts that we have, and thoughts are more important than the words we use to express them.

normal form & same

The normal form of an expression is the most direct way of writing it.

那要如何寫出normal form呢? (evaluation strategy)
這就是後面的問題了

Any two expressions that are the same have identical normal forms, and
any two expressions with identical normal forms are the same.
Sameness is always according to a type, so normal forms are also determined by a type.

ill-typed & type normal form

Expressions that cannot be described by a type and that are not themselves types are also called ill-typed.

以下面的code做例子

這最後會產出 (Pair 'olive 'oil)，WTF
這不是type也不是value，WTF
這就是ill-typed

而下面的code就是type了

從剛剛上面，就可以知道有時候type也是走一段路才知道是不是type
所以type也有normal form

Every expression that is a type has a normal form, which is the most direct way of writing that type.
If two expressions are the same type, then they have identical normal forms, and
if two types have identical normal forms, then they are the same type.

constructor & values

An expression with a constructor at the top is called a value.
Values are also called canonical expressions

8 is normal because its top, add1 is a constructor
because the argument tucked under the top add1, namely 7, is normal.

在定義normal form時，說normal form就是最直接寫出來的方式

那什麼是最直接?

constructor在頂部，同時其參數都是normal form

都是constructor在頂部，values 與 normal form差在哪裡

value的參數不一定是normal form
normal form連其參數都是normal form

evaluation & normal expression

Finding a value that is the same as some starting expression is called evaluation.

A normal expression has no remaining opportunities for evaluation.

Everything is expression

在dependent type中，大家都是expression

evaluation & value

在dependent type中，evaluation得到的是expression
在一般PL(像lisp)中，evaluation得到的是value
在一般PL中，expression與value是不同類別的東西

the meaning of an expression & evaluation

Q:
Doesn’t evaluation refer to finding the meaning of an expression, not just some simpler expression?

A:
Expressions do not refer to some external notion of meaning
In Pie, there is nothing but expressions and what we judge about them.

type constructor & data

Nat is not a constructor. zero and add1 are constructors that create data,
while Nat describes data that is just zero,or data that has add1 at its top and another Nat as its argument.

Pair is a type constructor because it constructs a type

Ch2

Eliminators

Eliminators take apart the values built by constructors. ex: car,cdr

一種看待Eliminator的方式就是 eliminator 可以把藏在某個type的資料拉出來

像是function的Eliminator就是apply

neutral (& value)

Expressions that are not values and cannot yet be evaluated due to a variable are called neutral.

(cons y 'rutabaga)
is a value.

(cdr x)
is neutral.

Each variable is the same as itself, no matter what type it has.

前面提過same必需要知道type，但是neutral expression沒有type!!

所以

Each variable is the same as itself, no matter what type it has.

Q:
為什麼variable有這種待遇?

A:
因為要替換variable時，長得一樣的variable都是一起被換掉的

If two expressions have identical eliminators at the top and all arguments to the eliminators are the same,
then the expressions are the same.

Neutral expressions that are written identically are the same, no matter their type.

Recursion is not an option.

Recursion is not an option because every expression must have a value.

Some recursive definitions make it possible to write expressions that do not have values.
